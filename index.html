Biography

I'm a final-year Ph.D. candidate at the Department of Computer Science and Engineering, The Chinese University of Hong Kong (CUHK), under the supervision of Prof. Evangeline F.Y. Young since Fall 2017. Before joining CUHK, I received my Bachelor Degree of Computer Science and Technology from Zhejiang University (ZJU).

My research interests include physical design of VLSI circuits and related ML-based problems.
